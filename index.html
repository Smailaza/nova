Leave Your Footprints

Welcome to the charming city of Agadir, where breathtaking nature, rich culture, and authentic Moroccan hospitality await you. Explore its golden beaches, scenic valleys, and lively markets—let Agadir leave a mark on your heart!

know about some places before your travel

Featured Places in Agadir

Agadir Beach

One of the most beautiful beaches in Morocco, stretching over 10 km—perfect for swimming, water sports, or just soaking in the sun.

Paradise Valley

A stunning natural escape in the mountains, with crystal-clear streams and palm-covered cliffs—ideal for hiking and natural swimming.

Agadir Oufella

The remains of the old fortress overlooking the city and the Atlantic Ocean. A must-visit for sunset views and city panoramas.

Crocoparc

A fun family destination featuring various crocodile species, lush gardens, and exotic plants.

Souk El Had

The largest traditional market in Agadir—immerse yourself in a true Moroccan experience full of colors, scents, and local crafts.

The Marina & Port

A scenic spot for walking, dining on fresh seafood, or watching the traditional fishing boats at work.
